隐私说明 / Privacy Policy

🔐 隐私承诺

Nano Banana 高度重视用户隐私保护。本隐私说明阐述了我们会如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

📸 收集的信息

我们仅收集提供服务所必需的信息：

  • 上传的图片：用于AI图像处理，处理完成后会自动删除
  • 生成记录：保存您的生成历史以便您查看和重用
  • 技术信息：设备类型、浏览器版本等匿名数据

🛡️ 数据安全

我们采取严格的安全措施保护您的数据：

  • 图片数据在传输和存储过程中进行加密
  • 生成记录仅保存在您的本地设备中
  • 不收集个人身份信息

🗑️ 数据保留

您的数据保留政策：

  • 上传的原始图片：处理完成后立即删除
  • 生成的图片：保存在您的本地浏览器中
  • 您可以随时清除浏览器数据来删除所有记录

📧 联系我们

如对隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 info@nanobanana.bar 联系我们。

🔐 Privacy Commitment

Nano Banana takes user privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

📸 Information Collected

We only collect information necessary to provide our services:

  • Uploaded Images: For AI processing, automatically deleted after processing
  • Generation History: Saved locally for your reference and reuse
  • Technical Information: Anonymous data like device type, browser version

🛡️ Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data:

  • Image data encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Generation history stored only on your local device
  • No personal identifiable information collected

🗑️ Data Retention

Our data retention policy:

  • Original uploaded images: Deleted immediately after processing
  • Generated images: Stored in your local browser
  • You can clear browser data at any time to remove all records

📧 Contact Us

For any questions about our privacy policy, contact us at info@nanobanana.bar.
